Cookie details

How cookies and similar technologies may be used.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files or pieces of data that may be stored on your browser or device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, or scripts that help websites operate and understand usage.

How AegisHome Uses Cookies

AegisHome may use cookies to support basic website features, remember cookie consent choices, improve site performance, understand general browsing behavior, and maintain a smoother experience across pages.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies or local storage items may be used to make the website work properly, keep the cookie banner from reappearing after a choice is made, support navigation, and maintain basic page behavior.

Preference Cookies

Preference technologies may remember choices such as cookie banner actions or website interaction preferences. These help the website feel more consistent when you return or move between pages.

Analytics and Performance Cookies

Analytics-related technologies may help AegisHome understand general website usage, such as visited pages, device type, browser type, approximate interaction patterns, and performance issues. These insights may be used to improve the website experience.

Request and Communication Support

Some technologies may support form behavior, request workflows, communication tools, spam prevention, or website reliability when visitors contact AegisHome or submit a home security request.

Third-Party Technologies

The website may use third-party tools for analytics, scripts, fonts, maps, form handling, communication support, or general site functionality. These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Cookie Banner Choice

The cookie banner includes Accept and Cancel options. Once you make a choice, AegisHome may store that choice using local storage so the banner does not keep appearing on every page visit.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies through browser settings. If you disable cookies or local storage, some features may not work as expected, including saved preferences or consent choices.

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers may send “Do Not Track” signals. Because there is no uniform industry standard for responding to these signals, the website may not respond to them in a specific way.
