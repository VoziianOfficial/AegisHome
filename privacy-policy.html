Privacy Policy

Learn how AegisHome handles request information, cookies, communication details, and privacy choices on this independent provider-matching platform.

Effective Date: June 19, 2026 Platform policy

Privacy overview

AegisHome uses information to help organize and support home security requests.

This Privacy Policy explains how AegisHome may collect, use, share, and protect information when visitors use the website, submit a request, browse service pages, or contact the platform.

AegisHome is not a home security installer, retailer, repair company, inspection company, monitoring provider, or emergency response service. It is an independent provider-matching platform that helps homeowners compare local provider options.

Platform note

Submitting a request does not guarantee provider availability, pricing, response time, service eligibility, or final project terms. Homeowners remain responsible for reviewing provider details before making decisions.

Policy details

How information may be collected and used.

Information You Provide

AegisHome may collect information that you choose to provide through forms or communications, such as your name, email address, phone number, ZIP code or service area, selected service category, and message details about your home security request.

Website Usage Information

The website may collect general usage information such as browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring pages, approximate interaction times, and cookie-related information to help improve site performance and user experience.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to requests, organize homeowner inquiry details, support provider-matching workflows, improve website functionality, prevent misuse, communicate with users, and maintain internal records.

Provider-Matching Use

If you submit a request, AegisHome may use request details to help identify relevant home security service categories or local provider options. The platform does not control provider pricing, availability, licensing, insurance, response times, or final service terms.

Information Sharing

AegisHome may share information with service providers, operational partners, analytics tools, communication tools, or matching-related partners as needed to support the website and request process. Information may also be shared when required by law or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember preferences, understand general site usage, improve performance, and support basic functionality. You can review more details on the Cookie Policy page.

Read Cookie Policy

Data Security

AegisHome uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. However, no website, internet transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request, adjust cookie settings in your browser, or contact AegisHome about communication preferences. Some website features may not function the same way if cookies are disabled.

Children’s Privacy

The website is intended for adults seeking information about home security service categories and provider options. AegisHome does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

AegisHome may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions may be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website after updates means the revised policy applies.

Privacy contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy?

Contact AegisHome if you have questions about this policy, privacy choices, or the way request information is handled on the platform.
